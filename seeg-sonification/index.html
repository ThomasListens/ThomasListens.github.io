sEEG Sonification Toolkit

Translating amplitude, morphology & frequency response of EEG into audio.

Pipeline

EDF/EDF+ → Preprocessing → 21 Features → Synthesis → Audio

Preprocessing: DC removal, notch (50/60 Hz), bipolar or CAR
Features: amplitude, spectral, waveform morphology, regularity, transient events
Synthesis: additive (root + overtones + undertones + FM)

Mapping

AmplitudeRMS, Hilbert envelope→ loudness
Polarityvoltage sign, complexity→ overtone/undertone balance
Frequencyspectral centroid, entropy→ FM depth, spectral range
DetectionPPAF, spectral flux→ transient emphasis
Outputline length, waveshaper→ gating, saturation, stereo
